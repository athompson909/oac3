- ABOUT US -

Welcome to our site! We are the Outdoor Adventure Crew, a group of friends that loves being outdoors, experiencing the world around us, and inspiring others to do the same.

Our goal is to help people understand that adventure is a state of mind not a destination, and that as such does not have to be expensive or extravagant. We want people to realize that you do not have to be a travel blogger, a mountain man or live out of a van to reap the benefits of the outdoors. YOU the student, the parent, the worker with a 9-5, the human, have a right to live life to it’s fullest, to explore the world around you, and to appreciate the beauty that surrounds us every day. Join us as we attempt to do the same, as we find beauty in our backyard and in turn find ourselves.

“We do not adventure to escape life, we adventure so that life does not escape us”

-The Crew
